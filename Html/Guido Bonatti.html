Guido Bonatti

Document

Guido Bonatti ( died between 1296 and 1300 ) was an Italian mathematician , astronomer and astrologer , who was the most celebrated astrologer of the 13th century . Bonatti was advisor of Frederick II , Holy Roman Emperor , Ezzelino da Romano III , Guido Novello da Polenta and Guido I da Montefeltro . He also served the communal governments of Florence , Siena and Forlì . His employers were all Ghibellines ( supporters of the Holy Roman Emperor ) , who were in conflict with the Guelphs ( supporters of the Pope ) , and all were excommunicated at some time or another . Bonatti 's astrological reputation was also criticised in Dante 's Divine Comedy , where he is depicted as residing in hell as punishment for his astrology . His most famous work was his Liber Astronomiae or ' Book of Astronomy ' , written around 1277 . This remained a classic astrology textbook for two centuries .
