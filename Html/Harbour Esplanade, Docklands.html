Harbour Esplanade, Docklands

Document

Harbour Esplanade is a waterfront street and thoroughfare in Docklands , Australia . It runs roughly north - south from Navigation Drive in the south to Docklands Drive in the north . The road also forms the eastern boundary of the Victoria Harbour inlet and is adjacent to Victoria Dock . The esplanade is a core element of the Docklands urban renewal precinct and is known for housing Docklands Stadium and several converted dock buildings that now serve as pubs and function spaces . Harbour Esplanade is also the location of office buildings and restaurants , whilst the Capital City Trail runs parallel along some of its length . It has been described as Melbourne 's waterfront boulevard and its future potential is compared to the successful Circular Quay in Sydney .
