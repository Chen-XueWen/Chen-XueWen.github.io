Lappeenranta ( ) is a city and municipality situated on the shore of the lake Saimaa in southeastern Finland , about from the Russian border . It belongs to the region of South Karelia . With approximately inhabitants ( ) Lappeenranta is the largest city in Finland . The neighboring municipality of Joutseno was consolidated with Lappeenranta on January 1 , 2009 , and the neighboring municipality of Ylämaa on January 1 , 2010 . Lappeenranta is known as an international university city in Finland with Lappeenranta University of Technology and Saimaa University of Applied Sciences which together have approximately 13,000 students from 68 countries . Lappeenranta is also a commercial centre of South - East Finland and the meeting point of the EU and Russia , 215   km from both Helsinki and St. Petersburg . Location on the southern shore of Lake Saimaa makes the city the region ’s centre for tourism . Lappeenranta is the second most visited city by Russian tourists in Finland after Helsinki and it competes with Helsinki for the largest share of tax - free sales in Finland . Lappeenranta is a model for renewable energies and a clean living environment . Lappeenranta was the only Finnish city among the 14 finalists in the international Earth Hour City Challenge 2014 , organized by WWF .
