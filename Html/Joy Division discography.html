Joy Division discography

Document

The discography of English rock band Joy Division consists of two studio albums , four live albums , twelve compilation albums , three extended plays , and five singles . The list does not include material performed by former members of Joy Division that was recorded as New Order ( formed by the surviving members of the band after the death of singer Ian Curtis ) or related side projects . Joy Division was formed in 1976 by guitarist Bernard Sumner and bassist Peter Hook , later recruiting singer Ian Curtis and drummer Stephen Morris . The band released its debut album , Unknown Pleasures , in 1979 on independent label Factory . On 18 May 1980 , the eve of the band 's first American tour , Curtis was found dead in his home . Unable to continue as Joy Division , the remaining members disbanded the group . The band 's second album , Closer , was released two months later to critical acclaim . Since then , several posthumous releases have been issued from the band .
