Louis Lombardi ( born January 17 , 1968 ) is an American actor . Lombardi was born in The Bronx , New York City , the son of Louis Lombardi , Sr . On television , Lombardi had a recurring role on The Sopranos as Agent Skip Lipari , and guest starred on such shows as Entourage , Heroes and . He was a cast member of a 1990s revival of Fantasy Island and played Edgar Stiles on 24 . He also played Stucky Fein in the short - lived television show Mob City . He has had roles in films including Beer League , The Usual Suspects , Natural Born Killers , Suicide Kings , Beverly Hills Cop III , The Animal , Spider - Man 2 , 3000 Miles to Graceland , The Crew , The Hot Chick , The Spirit , Battleship , and Jersey Boys . He also wrote and directed the film Dough Boys , released in 2008 .
