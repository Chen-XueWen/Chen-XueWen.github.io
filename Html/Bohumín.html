Bohumín

Document

Bohumín (; ; , ) is a town in Karviná District , Moravian - Silesian Region , Czech Republic on the border with Poland . The confluence of the Oder ( Odra ) and Olza rivers is situated just north of the town . The town lies in the historical region of Cieszyn Silesia . Bohumín consists of Old Bohumín and industrial New Bohumín , while several villages are administratively part of the town . The majority of citizens are Czech ; many citizens have Polish ancestry , although the Polish minority in Bohumín is small . Before World War II , the town was inhabited by a large German community . Nowadays the city is known as having one of the largest communities of Romani people in the Czech Republic .
