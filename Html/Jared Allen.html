Jared Scot Allen ( born April 3 , 1982 ) is a former professional American football defensive end . After playing college football for Idaho State University , he was drafted by the Kansas City Chiefs of the National Football League ( NFL ) in the fourth round of the 2004 NFL Draft . After four years with the Chiefs , Allen was traded to the Minnesota Vikings in exchange for three draft picks , including a first - rounder . He spent six years with the Vikings before joining the Chicago Bears as a free agent in 2014 . During the 2015 season , he was traded to the Carolina Panthers , with whom he played in his only career Super Bowl . A five - time Pro Bowl and four - time All - Pro selection , Allen tallied 136 quarterback sacks during his 12-year career .
