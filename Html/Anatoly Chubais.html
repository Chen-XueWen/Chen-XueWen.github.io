Anatoly Chubais

Anatoly Borisovich Chubais (; born 16 June 1955 ) is a Russian politician and businessman who was responsible for privatization in Russia as an influential member of Boris Yeltsin 's administration in the early 1990s . During this period , he was a key figure in introducing market economy and the principles of private ownership to Russia after the fall of the Soviet Union . From 1998 to 2008 , he headed the state - owned electrical power monopoly RAO UES . A 2004 survey conducted by PricewaterhouseCoopers and the Financial Times named him the world 's 54th most respected business leader . Currently , he is the head of the Russian Nanotechnology Corporation ( RUSNANO ) . He has been a member of the Advisory Council for JPMorgan Chase since September 2008 and a member of global board of advisers at the Council on Foreign Relations ( CFR ) since October 2012 .
