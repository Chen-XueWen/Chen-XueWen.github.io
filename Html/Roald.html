Roald is a village in Giske Municipality in Møre og Romsdal county , Norway . The village is located on the northern part of the island Vigra . Roald is about north of the city centre of Ålesund , connected via two undersea tunnels which opened in 1987 ( and will be going through extensive upgrades starting in 2008 ) . Ålesund Airport , Vigra is south of the village of Roald . Vigra Church is located a short distance south of Roald . The village of Roald has a population ( 2013 ) of 808 , giving the village a population density of . The village of Roald was the administrative centre of the old Roald Municipality that existed from 1890 until 1964 . The former municipality was later renamed Vigra Municipality . Since 1964 , it has been a part of Giske Municipality .
