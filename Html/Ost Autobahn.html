Ost Autobahn

Document

The ( Austrian ) Ost Autobahn A4 or " Eastern Motorway " is part of the European route E60 and goes from Vienna to the town of Nickelsdorf , on the Hungarian border . The road begins at the A23 at the Prater junction in Vienna , travels through Simmering , Schwechat , Vienna International Airport , Fischamend , Bruck an der Leitha , Parndorf , Weiden am See and Mönchhof to the border town of Nickelsdorf . Crossing the border into Hegyeshalom , the road becomes the Hungarian M1 motorway which then continues on to Budapest . The road is 66   km long . During the overflow of asylum seekers traveling from Hungary during September 2015 , the highway was closed intermittently preventing the traffic between Hungary and Austria , and it was reported that Austrian army was deployed on the border .
