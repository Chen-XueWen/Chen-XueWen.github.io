WUIS ( 91.9 FM ) , is the National Public Radio member station in Springfield , Illinois , United States . It primarily features National Public Radio news and talk programming . The station is owned by and based at the University of Illinois at Springfield . It operates a full - time satellite , WIPA in Pittsfield , Illinois . WIPA serves a small portion of the Quincy market . WUIS originally hit the airwaves on January 3 , 1975 as WSSR , operated by what was then Sangamon State University . It became WSSU in 1989 , and adopted its current calls when Sangamon State merged with the University of Illinois system in 1995 . WIPA was brought online in 1993 . In 2015 , the station rebranded as " NPR Illinois . "
