How Could Hell Be Any Worse?

Document

How Could Hell Be Any Worse ? is the first full - length album by American punk rock band Bad Religion , released on January 19 , 1982 by Epitaph Records . Released almost a year after their self - titled EP , it was financed by a $ 3,000 loan by guitarist Brett Gurewitz 's father . Its success surprised the band when it sold 10,000 copies in under a year . How Could Hell Be Any Worse ? was recorded over two time periods at Track Record Studios in North Hollywood , California , during October – November 1980 and again in January 1981 . After the original recording sessions , drummer Jay Ziskrout left Bad Religion and was replaced by his friend and the band 's roadie Pete Finestone , who was brought in to complete the rest of the album . Though not yet credited as a member of the band , future guitarist Greg Hetson , who was in Circle Jerks during this time , provided a guitar solo on " Part III " . How Could Hell Be Any Worse ? was also Bad Religion 's last album featuring Jay Bentley on bass for six years , until 1988 's Suffer . The front cover photograph was taken by Edward Colver near the Hollywood Bowl , while the back cover featured one of Gustave Dore 's illustrations of Dante 's Divine Comedy .
