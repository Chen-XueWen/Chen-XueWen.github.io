Mariana Cook

Document

Mariana Cook ( born 1955 ) is an American fine art photographer specializing in black and white photography and gelatin silver prints . Her work has been exhibited in the Metropolitan Museum of Art , the Museum of Modern Art , the J. Paul Getty Museum , the Boston Museum of Fine Arts , the National Portrait Gallery , the Bibliothèque Nationale , and the Musee d'Art Moderne . She is perhaps best known for her black and white portrait , A Couple in Chicago , which captures a young Barack and Michelle Obama in their 1996 Hyde Park apartment , and the accompanying interview for The New Yorker . Cook , the last surviving protégé of Ansel Adams , currently resides in New York City with her husband and daughter .
