Luis Roche

Document

Luis Roche was an important Venezuelan urbanist and was the Venezuelan ambassador to Argentina . He was also an amateur film director . In the 1930s , Roche advocated for the redesign of Caracas , to include developed corridors to a multi - purpose center . His suggestion involved a 36-meter - wide avenue based on Avenue des Champs - Élysées . He was responsible in great measure for the development of Caracas ' east side during the 1940s . He contributed to the development of neighborhoods such as La Castellana , Altamira and La Florida . Roche was a relevant diplomatic figure as well , being appointed as Venezuela 's ambassador to Argentina . He married a Colombian / French lady by the name of Beatrice Dugand , with whom he had four children , Beatrice Roche , Lilianne Roche , Marcel Roche and Luis Armando Roche . An important avenue in Altamira ( Caracas ) bears the name Luis Roche in his honor .
