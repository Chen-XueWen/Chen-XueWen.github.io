Vineeth Sreenivasan

Document

Vineeth Sreenivasan ( born 1 October 1985 ) is an Indian playback singer , actor , director , producer and screenwriter , who works in Malayalam cinema . Vineeth is son of the actor and screenwriter Sreenivasan . He made his film debut in 2003 as a playback singer with the song " Kasavinte Thattamittu " from Kilichundan Mampazham , and later also worked in music albums . Vineeth made his acting debut in Cycle ( 2008 ) , and since then he has acted in numerous films . His directorial debut was in 2010 with Malarvaadi Arts Club , also written by him . He scripted and directed the film Thira in 2013 , which introduced his younger brother Dhyan Sreenivasan as an actor . Vineeth 's second directorial venture Thattathin Marayathu ( 2012 ) became one of the highest grossing films of 2012 . His first script for another director was Oru Vadakkan Selfie ( 2015 ) , directed by G. Pajith . His most recent directorial was Jacobinte Swargarajyam ( 2016 ) . Vineeth established the film production house named Habit of Life and produced its debut film Aanandam in 2017 .
