Won - yong is a Korean masculine given name . Its meaning differs based on the hanja used to write each syllable of the name . There are 35 hanja with the reading " won " and 24 hanja with the reading " yong " on the South Korean government 's official list of hanja which may be registered for use in given names . Additionally , there is one character with the reading " ryong " ( , meaning " dragon " ) which may also be written and pronounced " yong " in South Korea .
