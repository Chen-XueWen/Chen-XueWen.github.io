Cove Lake State Park

Document

Cove Lake State Park is a state park in Campbell County , Tennessee , in the southeastern United States . The park consists of situated around Cove Lake , an impoundment of Cove Creek created by the completion of Caryville Dam in 1936 . The park 's location is adjacent to the town of Caryville and just west of Jacksboro . Cove Lake is an extension of the much larger Norris Reservoir , which extends across the lower of Cove Creek downstream from Caryville Dam . Cove Lake State Park was one of several state and local parks developed in the 1930s as part of the Norris Dam Project . The park includes a large campground , several small walking trails , and a wildlife observation area . A leg of the Cumberland Trail passes near the park 's northern boundary , connecting the park to nearby mountaintops .
